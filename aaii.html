Welcome to the AAAI India Chapter

2nd June, 2017

About AAAI: Founded in 1979, the Association for the Advancement of Artificial Intelligence (AAAI) is a nonprofit scientific society devoted to advancing the scientific understanding of the mechanisms underlying thought and intelligent behavior and their embodiment in machines. AAAI aims to promote research in, and responsible use of, artificial intelligence. More details can be found at http://aaai.org/.

 

AAAI India Chapter: The field of AI has been growing at an enormous pace in the recent years with tremendous interest being showed both by academia and industry. It has become imperative that India as a country needs focused effort in this direction if we have to sustain as a key contributor. Taking these factors into consideration IIIT Hyderabad in collaboration with a number of eminent researchers across the country has initiated the effort of establishing a AAAI chapter for India.
